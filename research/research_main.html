My work is primarily in epistemology, although I'm also interested in issues in metaphysics, philosophy of language, and philosophy of science. Presently, I am starting work on a dissertation focused on the epistemology of ideology. I plan to investigate the nature of ideological beliefs, how they are formed, how they change, and whether they are justified.

I am also interested in the digital humanities, and am especially keen to explore the ways that digital methods can aid philosophical research. I have compiled a portfolio highlighting my experiences with DH which can be viewed by clicking the button below.
